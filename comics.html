Comics!

Ever since I was a kid, I've loved comic books. Everything to do with them, the heros that come from them, the movies that are made from them, games, shows, and finally the actual books. There is something so cool about having physical copies of the very stories that help inspire and create so many superhero games and shows and put them on the big screen. I get a huge kick out of consuming that kind of stuff. Call me a dork, I know. I have grown a nice collection over the years but recently I've gotten REALLY into it. I am now trying to collect certain comics and stories that have influenced me.
